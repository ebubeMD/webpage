Team Edufun STEM & Robotics center is more than a collection of permanent & volunteer hands. We are a dedicated, passionate team, and we blend traditional values of education, teaching & learning with progressive design-thinking, problem solving and analytical reasoning. Our combined Nigerian team and international support advisors is 88 percent female, and nearly 30 percent are graduates of Edufun’s flagship and Legacy Program, Bridging the STEM-Gender Gap also tagged, Mind the Gap Project. This vision and race combined with gender mirroring ensures critical empathy and cultural relevance at every juncture, key to Girl Pioneers’ success in STEM related careers and in preparation for the future of work.
